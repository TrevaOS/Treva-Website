FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions About Performance Marketing and Google Ads

Everything you need to know before you pick up the phone. See all FAQs

How much Google Ads budget do you manage at Treva?

Treva has managed over ₹15 Crore in Google Ads spend across industries including SaaS, eCommerce, real estate, education and healthcare in Bengaluru and across India.

What is performance marketing?

Performance marketing is a form of digital advertising where every rupee spent is tied to a measurable outcome such as a click, a lead, a sale or a customer. Unlike brand advertising, performance marketing campaigns are optimised in real-time based on data.

How long does it take to see results from Google Ads?

Most clients see improved CPL and ROAS within the first 4 to 6 weeks as the campaign optimises. Full performance typically stabilises within 90 days.

Why is my Google Ads cost per lead so high?

High CPL is usually caused by keyword sprawl, poor Quality Scores, broad match without negative keyword controls or a weak landing page. Treva audits all four in the account review.

Do you work with small businesses or only enterprises?

We work with growth-stage startups spending ₹50,000 per month and enterprise brands investing ₹50 Lakh or more per month. Our strategy scales to your budget.

Are you based in Bangalore?

Yes. Treva is based in Vijayanagar, Bengaluru. We serve clients across India and internationally.
