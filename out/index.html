Quick Answers

What does Treva do?+

Treva is a Bangalore-based 360° growth partner combining performance marketing (Google Ads, Meta), social media management, branding, web and app development, Treva CRM, Restaurant Dashboard, and Creator Hub UGC into one compounding system.

Is Treva a marketing agency or a tech company?+

Both. Treva runs campaigns and builds products. Our services and proprietary technology work together so your marketing data feeds your CRM, your CRM informs your ads, and your UGC content drives your social — all from one partner.

Does Treva work with businesses outside Bangalore?+

Yes. While Treva is headquartered in Bangalore, we work with brands across India. Creator Hub operates nationally.
