Our Story

From Bengaluru, Built for Results — The Treva Story

We decided to build something different. A performance marketing agency that operates more like a growth partner than a vendor. Where you speak directly to the strategist running your campaigns. Where every decision is tied back to one metric: does this improve your ROI?

Since then, we have managed over ₹15 Crore in Google Ads budget, served brands ranging from McKinsey-grade enterprises to bootstrapped D2C founders, and built a reputation in Bengaluru's marketing community for one thing: measurable results.

We are based in Vijayanagar, Bengaluru. We are not a 500-person agency. We are a focused team of Google Ads specialists who believe that depth beats breadth — every time.

Expertise

₹15 Crore in Ad Spend Managed. Here's What We Learned.

Most Google Ads accounts are structured to spend, not to convert

After auditing hundreds of accounts, the pattern is clear: campaigns are built to satisfy Google's spend recommendations, not the client's cost per lead goals.

The gap between ad click and business outcome is where money dies

A technically perfect ad pointing to a weak landing page is money wasted. Performance marketing includes the full funnel — not just the ad.

Data without strategy is just expensive reports

Dashboards full of impressions and clicks mean nothing if they are not tied to revenue. Every metric we report maps back to business outcome.

How We Work

Our Principles — Why Clients Stay With Treva Year After Year

We track everything or we do not run it

If we cannot measure it, we cannot optimise it. Every campaign comes with full conversion tracking set up before a single rupee is spent.

Your CPL target is our north star, not Google's suggested bid

Google's recommendations are designed to spend your budget, not protect your ROI. We set targets based on your business model — and hold ourselves accountable to them.

We tell you when something is not working — before you ask

Proactive communication is not optional. If a campaign underperforms, you hear from us first with a plan, not excuses.

The strategist running your account is the person you speak to

No account managers as middlemen. The expert optimising your Google Ads is the one answering your calls and emails.
